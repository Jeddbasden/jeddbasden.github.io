Get To Know Me

A little about me, I was born in Nassau, Bahamas. I enjoy playing sports, longboarding, sitting in my hammock watching sunsets, and ofcourse coding!. My love for coding grew through watching youtube videos. After learning some things through youtube videos and udemy courses, I made my first hello world and some simple python apps. When i had that first feeling of successfully getting a project to work I was hooked immediately. With that i decided to pursuit a career in software development.

Over the course of time i have gained a very solid grasp and understanding of a plethora of technologies and frameworks. Throughout my journey as a software engineer i have collaborated with multiple teams and partners on several projects and coding problems. So far the ups, downs, countless hours, lack of sleep, and morning coffee, have made for an amazing experience. I've learned so much and I'm hoping as i continue to grow and learn as software developer this site will to! Thank you for coming to my TED Talk.
